# overview

Omega (FC2.5) is the evolved version of FC2, a multi-module library designed for community contributions through Lua scripting or other approaches. FC2 originally contained multiple attached solutions, Omega consolidates them into a single executable.

This version introduces improvements in performance and security. All modules have been rewritten for improved organization. Omega marks the final major project of its type from constelia.ai. There will be no FC3, sequel, or reboot. The name "Omega" reflects its role as the ultimate form: a central hub integrating elements from 8 years of fantasy.cat's (constelia.ai) development. It combines FC2's security and platform diversification with its customization and flexibility, alongside security features from other solutions, all unified in one package.

## getting started

To use Omega or any service from constelia.ai (fantasy.cat), you need to be a verified member. If you are verified, check the [guide](https://constelia.ai/forums/index.php?threads/omega-%CE%A9.11298/). 

After the history roadmap, look for the "Table of Contents" section at the bottom of the post. It has a list of detailed info on each solution, security features, troubleshooting, scripting, and more.
